Privacy Policy for Fatality Post Tracker

Effective date: 15 September 2025

This app, Fatality Post Tracker, is designed to read publicly available posts from selected Facebook pages for personal use and research purposes. No private user data is collected or shared with third parties.

Data collected by this app includes only publicly available posts, reactions, comments, and share counts from the pages accessed.

By using this app, you acknowledge that only public data is used, and no personal Facebook account information is accessed beyond what is publicly visible.

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer via the email associated with this app.
